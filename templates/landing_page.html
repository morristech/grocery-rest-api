About

The purpose of this site is to allow access to Grocery Items RESTful APIs for front-end developers.

If you are a front-end developer (mobile or web) and looking to consume APIs for your application

then Grocery Items RESTful APIs is the right choice.

And the good news is it is FREE!

Testing

Postman is one way to test the APIs. It is the most commonly used method nowadays.

All the Grocery Items RESTful APIs can be accessed through GET method.

Also, the request header Content-Type can be specified with application/json value.

The next section lists down different request URLs of Grocery Items RESTful APIs

that can be accessed through Postman.

Explore APIs
